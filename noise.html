Noise Pollution Awareness

What is Noise Pollution?

Noise pollution is the excessive, displeasing, or disruptive noise that interferes with normal activities. It can have detrimental effects on health and well-being.

Causes of Noise Pollution

  • Traffic noise
  • Industrial activities
  • Construction sites
  • Social events

Effects of Noise Pollution

Common effects include stress, hearing loss, sleep disturbances, and adverse impacts on wildlife.
